What a student learns in class 6 Mathematics?


The beginning of Class 6 Maths is initiated by introducing a child to new concepts of numbers.

CH-1
Class 6 Maths Chapter 1 “Knowing Our Numbers” tells about numbers, their multiplication, division, number names, patterns, difference between digits and the numbers, place value system, etc. Further the understanding of the child is developed using different measuring units. Process and applications of Estimation has been described to a student in such a way that they can relate it to daily life examples.

CH-2
Class 6 Maths Chapter 2 “Whole Numbers” discusses the properties of whole numbers which helps students to understand the mathematical operations in a better way. Divisibility tests and concepts of HCF and LCM are developed in a better way.

CH-3
Class 6 Maths Chapter 3 “Playing with Numbers involves the understanding of dealing with brackets during Addition, Subtraction, multiplication and division.

CH-4
Class 6 Maths Chapter 4 “Basic Geometrical Ideas” introduces the basic knowledge about angles, lines and how a shape depends upon these factors.

CH-5
Class 6 Maths Chapter 5 “Understanding Elementary Shapes” discusses broad categorisation of the shapes formed using curves and lines.

CH-6
Class 6 Maths Chapter 6 “Integers” is the knowledge about a new family of numbers that has been introduced for the first time.

CH-7
Class 6 Maths Chapter “Fractions”focuses on what are different forms of numbers and how to deal with them and its application in daily life.

CH-8
Class 6 Maths Chapter 8 “Decimals” focuses on what are different forms of numbers and how to deal with them and its application in daily life.

CH-9
Class 6 Maths Chapter 9 “Data Handling” comes up with bar graphs and pie charts and adds interest to the learning process of the student.

CH-10
Class 6 Maths Chapter 10 “Mensuration” explains the concepts of Area and Perimeter with the help of things surrounding the child’s life.

CH-11
Class 6 Maths Chapter 11 “Algebra” teaches them the basic meaning of Algebra, what are variables and their significance.

CH-12
Class 6 Maths Chapter 12 “Ratio and Proportion” introduces the meaning of terms ratio and proportion and its application in making comparisons.

CH-13
Class 6 Maths Chapter 13 “Symmetry” is all about symmetrical shapes and understanding the division between different patterns and shapes.

CH-14
Class 6 Maths Chapter 14 Practical Geometry makes a child familiar with different geometrical tools and in the way of doing basic constructions.

The textbook of Class 6 Maths all chapters guides teachers as well as students to understand the concept, reason behind the process rather than just memorizing it. Many opportunities are provided in textbooks where children will be verifying principles or patterns.


What Students study in class 6 science?


The aim of class 6 science book is to make a textbook companion for a child. Paheli and Boojho are the two characters who accompany the child throughout the textbook and help them to engage more with the content, questions, activities etc. class 6 science all chapters are comprehensive and interesting.

CH-1
Class 6 science Chapter 1 discusses the different sources of food, plant parts and animal products like food and what animals eat.

CH-2
Class 6 science chapter 2 informs about the components of food, balanced diet, various nutrients and their requirements in the body.

CH-3
class 6 science Chapter 3 Fibre to Fabric tells about the different variety of fabrics, sources of different fibres, their formation and history of clothing material.

CH-4
Class 6 Science Chapter 4 introduces a child to the concept and importance of classification. It tells about different materials around us and their properties.

CH-5
Class 6 Science Chapter 5 deals with techniques of separation of different states of matter.

CH-6
Class 6 Science Chapter 6 is a brief introduction to physical and chemical changes.

CH-7
Class 6 Science Chapter 7 is about getting to know more about plants. It informs about different herbs, shrubs and trees and their various components.

CH-8
Class 6 Science Chapter 8 deals with different Body Movements. It explains bones, joints and the structure of animals.

CH-9
class 6 science Chapter 9 deals with the organisms and the surroundings where they live, habitat and adaptation, different life processes.

CH-10
Class 6 Science Chapter 10 introduces the new concept of Motion and different techniques of measurement of distance.

CH-11
Class 6 Science Chapter 11 again brings along a new concept of Light, shadows and reflection.

CH-12
Class 6 Science Chapter 12 is an introduction to the concept of electricity, how it is produced and what are circuits.

CH-13
Class 6 Science Chapter 13 deals with the concept of magnets, how they were discovered, what are magnetic and non-magnetic materials. All these chapters will help a child to understand the different phenomenon and the reasons for the things they usually see around.

CH-14
Class 6 Science Chapter 14 is all about resources of water, water cycle, ways to preserve water.

CH-15
Class 6 Science Chapter 15 provides some information about the composition of air, availability of oxygen, carbon-di-oxide, nitrogen and other gases.

CH-16
Class 6 Science Chapter 16 tells about the importance of the drainage system, how to deal with garbage and recycling methods.

The syllabus of social studies is divided into three major parts namely History, Civics and Geography. In class 6 SST, all chapters are formulated to enhance student’s basic awareness and lifestyle.

Geography – The Earth Our Habitat
What all chapters are combined in Class 6 Geography?


CH-1
Class 6 sst geography Chapter 1, The Earth in Our Solar System focuses on learning about the sun, planets, and some celestial bodies that form the solar system.

CH-2
Class 6 sst geography Chapter 2 focuses on the child becoming familiar with the Globe latitudes and longitudes.

CH-3
Class 6 sst geography Chapter 3 provides the meaning of rotation and revolution is given and the child learns about Motions of Earth.

CH-4
Class 6 sst geography Chapter 4 describes the need and use of maps.

CH-5
Class 6 sst geography chapter 5 explains The Domains of Earth namely - lithosphere, atmosphere, hydrosphere and biosphere.

CH-6
In Class 6 sst geography Chapter 6 The Landforms of Earth are explained.

CH-7&8
At last, Class 6 sst geography Chapter 7 and Chapter 8 focus on location, climate, vegetation and wildlife of India.

Class 6 SST all chapter pdf are available of all the chapters at EKAL shiksha for free which promotes practical learning.

History - Our Past I
What class 6 History book is all about?
CH-1
While studying, Class 6 SST history Chapter 1 answers the questions of What, where, how and when about the past.

CH-2
Class 6 SST history Chapter 2 evolution of humans is explained from being hunters and gatherers to growing food on their own.

CH-3
Class 6 SST history Chapter 3 informs about the establishment of the Earliest Cities.

CH-4
Class 6 SST history Chapter 4 talks about an important source of history The Books and the Burials which helps us to know more about the past.

CH-5
Then, Class 6 SST history Chapter 5 tells about the Kingdoms, Kings, and an Early Republic. It explains about the administrative system in the past.

CH-6
Class 6 SST history Chapter 6 concentrates on the new questions and the ideas that prevailed in the past related to religion, language etc.

CH-7
Class 6 SST history Chapter 7 is based on the story of a Great Emperor Ashoka and his Empire.

CH-8
Class 6 SST history Chapter 8 focuses on establishment of Vital Villages and Thriving Towns.

CH-9
Next, Class 6 SST history chapter 9 discusses the establishment of trade, traders, kings and the pilgrims.

CH-10&11
At last, Class 6 SST history Chapter 10 and 11 are based upon the New Empires and Kingdoms and the buildings, paintings and books of that period.

All the chapters in NCERT class 6 SST contain a short text, storyline which will help a child to relate all the concepts of past with the present time. Class 6 History all chapter PDF will help them to understand how things were in the past and how they have changed now.

Civics- Social and Political Life I
What all topics are covered in class 6 Civics ( Social and Political life I)?
Class 6 sst civics includes various chapters that discuss social and political life deeply with respect to communal issues.

CH-1&2
Class 6 Civics Chapter 1 and 2 introduces the child the meaning and concept of Diversity and the Discrimination people have to face.

CH-3&4
Next, Class 6 Civics Chapter 3 and 4 focuses on the meaning of Government and the key elements of Democratic Government.

CH-5
Class 6 Civics Chapter 5 is all about the Panchayati Raj.

CH-6,7,8,9
Class 6 Civics chapter 6, 7, 8 and 9, the child learns about the differences in livelihood and administration of Rural and Urban Areas.

Class 6 SST all chapter pdf are available for student’s ease for a better insight and growth that carries forward their future with good marks.
